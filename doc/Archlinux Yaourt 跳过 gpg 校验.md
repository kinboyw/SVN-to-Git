```shell
yaourt --m-arg "--skippgpcheck" -S {{ package }}
```



> ###
>
>  --skipinteg           
>
> ​	Do not perform any integrity checks (checksum and PGP)           on source files.            
>
> --skipchecksums           
>
> ​	Do not verify checksums of source files.           
>
>  --skippgpcheck           
>
> ​	Do not verify PGP signatures of source files
